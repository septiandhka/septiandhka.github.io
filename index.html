About Me

I am an informatics engineering student from Muhammadiyah Jakarta University, currently I'm in my 4th semester. By taking this major, I have the opportunity to combine creativity with technology. I am interested in developing innovative solutions, applications and systems that can have a positive impact on people's lives. I can learn about UI/UX design, software development, and concepts that will allow me to turn creative ideas into reality.
I also have a passion for Arts. I find joy in listening to my music while singing on it, drawing the moment, taking photos and everything. For me, art is a way to communicate and express myself. I believe that the collaboration of my interest of Art with my knowledge of Technology can provide a unique perspectives, creative solutions while bringing the necessary technical acumen to the team and innovation to the bussines world.
